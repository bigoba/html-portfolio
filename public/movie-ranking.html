My top 3 Movies of all-time

The Brotherhood of War (2004)

The Brotherhood of War is a sweeping South Korean war epic that follows two brothers forcibly drafted into the Korean War, exploring themes of sacrifice, brotherhood, and the devastating impact of conflict on family.
